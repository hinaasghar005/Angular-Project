Safer Gambling

The fairest way to play

At Betfair, we've always believed betting should be fun and fair for everyone.

To us, fairness means shining a light on the potential risks of playing and betting, and providing tools and support to help make
sure that everyone plays in a way that suits them.

That’s what this website is for – a hub to find out all about the Safer Gambling tools that help give you control over your play, ways
to get in touch, and signs to watch out for so you can stay one step ahead.
